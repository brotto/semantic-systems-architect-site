# SSA Glossary

- Semantic Systems Architect (SSA): professional who designs computer systems via language-oriented semantic modeling.
- Domain ontology: formal structure of entities, relationships and rules of the domain.
- Semantic contract: explicit agreement on meaning, format and expected behavior.
- Context engineering: discipline of designing context to guide model behavior.
- Semantic interface: integration point defined by intention and meaning, not just technical type.
- Handoff: formal transfer of state/responsibility between agents.
- Guardrail: behavioral risk containment mechanism.
- Evals: set of assessments to measure quality and regression.
- Semantic regression: worsening of conceptual behavior after change.
- Semantic on-call: operation focused on AI behavior incidents.
