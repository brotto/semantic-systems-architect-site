# 30-60-90 Plan for SSA Onboarding

## Days 1-30

- Align SSA taxonomy and company principles.
- Select priority cases.
- Train fundamentals and engineering context.
- Define quality and risk baseline.

## Days 31-60

- Run applied laboratories in pilot squads.
- Implement evals and semantic observability.
- Validate security and governance policies.

## Days 61-90

- Deliver 1 capstone per pilot squad.
- Measure impact on cost, quality and speed.
- Define quarterly roster plan.

## Minimum deliverables

- Internal SSA roadmap.
- Catalog of approved standards.
- Maturity matrix by squad.
