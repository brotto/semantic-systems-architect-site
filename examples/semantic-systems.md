# Semantic Systems Examples

## 1. Clinical triage copilot
- semantic inputs: symptoms, risk factors, patient context
- semantic outputs: risk classification, escalation decision, rationale
- guardrails: mandatory escalation under high-risk patterns

## 2. Contract risk analyst
- semantic inputs: clauses, jurisdiction, policy constraints
- semantic outputs: risk map, confidence, required human review
- guardrails: no legal assertion without explicit source citation

## 3. Enterprise support orchestrator
- semantic inputs: ticket context, account criticality, incident signals
- semantic outputs: priority, troubleshooting plan, handoff path
- guardrails: escalation to human operations for severe incidents
